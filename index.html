FAQs

When will you be graduating from SDSU?

I will be graduating from SDSU in Spring 2024.

What do you have planned for after graduation?

After graduation, I plan on taking a nice break with family and friends and then come back to officially start my career. Specifically, I plan to find a job in what encompasses the creative field of Multimedia.

What was your favorite class you took at SDSU?

My favorite class I took at SDSU was Screen Printing, (ART 313). Apart from doing more digital work-related classes, it was a new experience for me to work with something more hands-on. Screen printing offers a unique blend of not only hands-on creativity but also technical skills, which made the class fun and engaging. It also had a very welcoming and collaborative atmosphere with an enjoyable professor, Scott Wyss, and friends to socialize with.

What work are you most proud producing at SDSU?

I'm proud of all the work I produced at SDSU, so it's hard to choose which one I am most proud of. Though, I would have to say Black and White Photography (ART 407) was something I’m proud of accomplishing. It was a very hands-on and tedious type of photography, which took a lot of time and effort. So, I was pleased that I was able to create something that I can say I gained new knowledge and skills.
